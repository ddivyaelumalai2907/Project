AMER FORT

Cradled on the top of a hill near Jaipur lies Amer Fort,one of the most maginificent palaces in Inadia.Also commonly known as Amber fort.The majestic building with its maze-like passagesand serpentine staircase is an architectural masterpiece and with significant importance in Indian history!!It's only 11kms away from Jaipur ,Amer Fort is clad in pink and yellow sandstone and the part of extensive complex,the Amer Fort was inducted into UNESCO World Heritage site list as part of the "Hills Forts of Rajasthan "along another five forts. Mughal architecture greatly influenced the architectural style of several buildings of the fort.Constructed of red sandstone and marble, the attractive, opulent palace is laid out on four levels, each with a courtyard.It consists of the Diwan-e-Aam, or "Hall of Public Audience", the Diwan-e-Khas, or "Hall of Private Audience", the Sheesh Mahal (mirror palace),
or Jai Mandir, and the Sukh Niwas where a cool climate is artificially created by winds that blow over a water cascade within the palace.
